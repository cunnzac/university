The Best Movies According to Zachery

My top 3 movies

I'm Thinking of Ending Things

You can watch this movie on Netflix, it's based on a book (same title), but I prefer the movie. Something about this film lights a weird type of fire in my soul, hah.

Pearl

I will always defend Pearl, even if she was a murderer, I blame the circumstances she grew up in. The "I'm a star" scene (vague to avoid spoilers) is heartbreaking. My favorite part was her monologue at the end.

Coraline

Admittedly, this movie landing in my top three has a lot to do with it's popularity online. I hadn't paid much attention to it as a kid, but watching YouTube videos explaining the lore helped me see how cool this movie is.
